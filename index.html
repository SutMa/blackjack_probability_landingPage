About

Learning Mode

An interactive exploration of blackjack probability. Users can pick starting hand for both player and dealer. Guess the probability of winning. Choose to hit or stand, and win points. These points can be used to purchase new card designs.

Game Mode

Simulates a real blackjack game, where players can hit or stand. The dealer (machine) will then reveal their "hole" card If the dealer busts, you win. If not, the dealer's hand is compared to yours.

Learn

Blackjack is a card game played between one player and a dealer, with the objective of having a hand value closer to 21 than the dealer's without exceeding it. Players receive two initial cards and can choose to 'hit' to get more cards or 'stand' to keep their current hand. The dealer must follow specific rules, usually hitting until they have 17 or higher. Winning occurs when the player's hand is closer to 21 than the dealer's without busting, with additional rewards for a blackjack (an Ace and a 10-value card).
